FANTA ESPORTS is the new game that allows you to create a complete Pro Players League of Legends team from scratch and have it fight in a League formed by the users themselves.

At the beginning of the creation of the competition, players choose the professional league of reference (LEC, LCS, LPL or LCK) and the number of players who will participate, which varies from 2 to 10 users.

Then the payment, chosen by the participants, will define the cash prizes up for grabs: the higher the entry fee, the higher the prizes.

Once all users have settled the payment, you get to the heart of the game. Depending on the professional league of reference you will have a pool of players selectable for your team, which are precisely the Pro Playersthat play there.

Each participant will have a budget of 100 FE-credits usable to buy players for each team. Draft includes a complete roster of players: Toplaner, Jungler, Midlaner, ADC, Support and a variable role substitute (given the possible changes of roster within the real teams). Each player will have an updated and balanced credit value every year according to their performance in regional and international tournaments.

Once you have completed your team of champions, you will pass to clashes:each League will have a duration equal to the reference competition (for example 18 weeks in the case of LEC) and the scores will be calculated according to the real performance of the players in their league.

At the end of the competition, the team of each player will have reached a total score that will be the sum of the individuals (to check the score is provided for the appropriate SCORES section) and, clearly, who will have the highest score within the League will come first, the second score in second place and so on.

In the case of drastic changes of roster or the forced lack of a player, the credits spent for an absent player will be refunded and it will be possible buy a new player to return to compete better (depending on how many FE-credits remained to the user).

It will be possible change your roster every week before the competitive days begin, but it will not be possible change your team during the days in which the games are played.

CREATE YOUR OWN LEAGUE AND HAVE FUN WITH YOUR FRIENDS!
