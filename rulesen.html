Depending on the performances in the respective leagues, Pro Players will have a personal score, which reflected in FANTA ESPORTS, can make the difference within the online competition.


INDIVIDUAL SCORES

2 points for each kill

-1.5 points for each death

1 point for each assist

0.01 points for minion killed

2 points for each triple kill

5 points for each quadra kill

10 points for each penta kill

2 points if a player accumulates 10 or more assists or kills in the same match


SCORES AS A TEAM

2 points for every win

2 points for every Baron Nashor killed

1 point for every Dragon killed

2 points for each First Blood

1 point for each tower destroyed

-1 point for each tower lost
