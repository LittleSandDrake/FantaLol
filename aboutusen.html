FANTA ESPORTS is an idea born of two guys from Parma (Italy), League of Legends players, who want to covey their passion for this game. The idea behind our project is to bring as much people as possible to the competitive world in the most fun way possible: challenging friends.

The game is not just competition, but, in full spirit Riot Games, create a community where to share an important piece of our lives as this game can be.

The innovative aspect of the world of League is to become ourselves "manager" of a virtual competitive team and make it compete, something that has been present for some time in various sports such as football or basketball. In these fictitious competitions we create companies of friends that last even several years, and this is what we want to create with FANTA ESPORTS.

We hope with our game to get you passionate about League of Legends and its world.

Create your own leagues and create new ties with FANTA ESPORTS!

The Fanta eSports Team
